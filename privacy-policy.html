3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under GDPR Article 6(1)(a) — Consent. You give explicit consent by ticking the checkbox before submitting your details. You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 7).

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you your personalised ROI report via email
  • To send occasional emails about AI automation tips and services (only if you consented)
  • To improve our tools and services (aggregated, non-identifying analysis only)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in our CRM system (GoHighLevel), which is hosted in the United States. GoHighLevel maintains appropriate data protection measures including encryption in transit and at rest. By consenting, you acknowledge this transfer outside the UK/EEA under GDPR Article 49(1)(a).

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for as long as you remain subscribed to our communications. If you withdraw consent or request deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent at any time
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email grant@aifusionautomations.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data rights have been violated.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our free tools pages do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. No third-party fonts or scripts that transfer your IP address to external services are loaded. All processing happens in your browser.

9. Third-Party Links

Our tools may contain links to external websites (e.g. our main website at aifusionautomations.com). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent changes. Continued use of our tools after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
